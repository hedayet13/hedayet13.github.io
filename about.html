About Me

My name is Md. Hedayetul Islam Chy, and I have recently completed my Master of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Chittagong, where I was advised by Dr. Mohammed Arif Iftakher Mahmood . My research focused on bio-inspired surface design, specifically exploring natural structures to develop anti-splashing surfaces that mimic biological forms and improve fluid interaction efficiency. My broader academic interests include Cyber-Physical Systems, Intelligent Automation, Trustworthy AI, Computer Vision, and Bio-Engineering.

Projects

Design, Fabrication & Performance Analysis of 3D Anti-Splashing Surface

Aim: We're currently crafting a cutting-edge surface as a key component of our ongoing research endeavor. This innovative surface is meticulously designed to minimize liquid splashes. Leveraging advanced fluid physics simulations, particularly with the support of Ansys Fluent, we've fine-tuned multiple parameters to perfection. Our exploration has led us to utilize top-tier materials like SLA-resin and PDMS, ensuring the utmost effectiveness and durability of the structure.

Tools: Ansys Fluent, CFD, SolidWorks 3D, OriginPro
